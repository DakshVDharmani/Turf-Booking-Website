Frequently Asked Questions


Your TurfConnect Queries Answered

Find answers to common questions about booking turfs, using our platform, and more!

FAQs

How do I book a turf on TurfConnect?

Navigate to the "Book Turf Slot" page, select your preferred location, date, and time, and follow the prompts to confirm your booking. You’ll receive a confirmation email with details.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify bookings up to 24 hours before the slot via your account dashboard. Refunds depend on the turf’s cancellation policy.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking. All payments are processed securely through our trusted payment gateway.

How do I purchase sports equipment?

Visit the "Equipments and More" page, add items to your cart, and complete the purchase using our secure checkout process.

Is there a membership program?

Yes, our premium membership offers discounts on bookings, exclusive events, and priority slots. Check the "Home" page for details.

How do I track my sports stats?

Use the "Cricket Stats" or "My Sports Development" pages to input match data and view analytics like scorecards and shot ranges.

What sports are supported on TurfConnect?

Currently, we support cricket, with plans to add soccer, badminton, and more in future updates.

How does the TurfTalk chatbot work?

Click the chatbot icon to start a 24/7 conversation for booking help, sports tips, or platform navigation.

Can I list my turf on TurfConnect?

Yes, turf owners can register on our platform to list venues. Contact us via the "Contact Us" page for onboarding details.

Are there age restrictions for booking?

Users must be 18+ to book independently. Minors can book with parental consent, subject to turf policies.

How do I join a community game?

Check the "Home" page for open games in your area, sign up, and connect with players via our chat feature.

What if a turf is unavailable?

Use our search filters to find alternative turfs nearby or contact our support team for assistance.

Can I book multiple slots at once?

Yes, you can book multiple slots in one session, subject to availability and turf policies.

How are refunds processed?

Refunds are credited to your original payment method within 5-7 business days, per the turf’s refund policy.

Is my personal data secure?

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Read our "Privacy Policy" for more details.

How do I report an issue with a turf?

Use the "Contact Us" page or chat with TurfTalk to report issues, and we’ll coordinate with the turf owner.

Can I rate or review a turf?

Yes, after your booking, you can rate and review the turf on your account dashboard to help other players.

Are group discounts available?

Some turfs offer group discounts. Check the turf’s booking page or contact us for details.

How do I update my profile?

Go to your account settings to update your name, contact details, and preferences.

What if I forget my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it via email.

Still Have Questions?

Reach out to our support team or chat with our TurfTalk chatbot for instant assistance!
